## Evaluation Criteria
1. **Object Accuracy**: Correct objects, proportions, positioning?
2. **Style Match**: Correct visual style (realistic/low-poly/cartoon), appropriate materials?
3. **Material Quality**: Correct colors, surface properties (metallic/roughness)?
4. **Completeness**: Missing parts? Errors or artifacts?
5. **Overall Quality**: Recognizable? Visually appealing?

- 1-3 **FAIL**: Inadequate representation
- 0 **FAIL**: Complete failure or empty
